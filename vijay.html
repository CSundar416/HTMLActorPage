vijay

actor vijay born on 22 june 1974 in chennai,tamil nadu,india. he is an indian film actor and playback singer who works primarily in tamil cinema. he has also appeared in some telugu, hindi, and kannada films. vijay is one of the highest-paid actors in india and has a massive fan following. he is known for his charismatic screen presence, dance skills, and versatile acting abilities. some of his popular films include "thuppakki," "mersal," "sarkar," and "bigil." vijay has won several awards for his performances and continues to be a prominent figure in the indian film industry.
